About us

At Calvino's bike repair shop, we pride ourselves in giving you 110% effort when performing our due dilligence on repairing your bike. We are happy to answer any questions you have or have a chat with you about anything you choose to pass time. We have been in business for over 25 years and we are fully-versed in the bike-repairing field.

Our service costs do not include the price of parts.

Meet our professional mechanic

Calvino

Calvino has a PhD in mechanical engineering and is a national champion cyclist. He has participated in the Tour de France race three times during his prime cycling years from 1999-2007.

Calvino is a skilled mechanic especially when it comes to fixing bikes. He has amassed over 20 years experience at diagnosing and repairing them. You will not regret paying for his services.
