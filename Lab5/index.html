About us

At Calvino's bike repair shop, we pride ourselves in giving you 110% effort when performing our due dilligence on repairing your bike. We are happy to answer any questions you have or have a chat with you about anything you choose to pass time. We have been in business for over 25 years and we are fully-versed in the bike-repairing field.
